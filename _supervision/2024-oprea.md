Daily supervision of Maria Oprea's Bachelor thesis, conducted under the academic supervision of Prof. Oliver Thomas and Dr. Simon Pukrop at Osnabr端ck University in collaboration with DFKI.

## Thesis Overview
This thesis explores the application of diffusion models to enhance the process of floor plan drafting. By leveraging the generative capabilities of diffusion models, the work aims to assist architects and designers in creating accurate and efficient floor plans, improving both the speed and quality of architectural design workflows.

The resulting model is available on Hugging Face: https://huggingface.co/maria26/Floor_Plan_LoRA
